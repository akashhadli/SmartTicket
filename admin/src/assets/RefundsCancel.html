successful by the application. However, subject to the user/commuter/passenger reasoning,
LekPay reserves the right to deny any refund to user/commuter/passenger pursuant to a
cancellation initiated by the user. If tickets/pass is not successful and the
user/commuter/passenger provides sufficient proof, the money may be refunded within 10 days,
as may be necessary at the sole discretion of LeyPay.
